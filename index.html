Project Overview

Our game is a university campus management simulator where players build academic buildings, dormitories, and recreational areas to create a comfortable environment for students and keep them satisfied. While managing budgets, players make decisions about new buildings and track resources. A round lasts five minutes of real time, corresponding to three game years, with the option to pause the game and adjust the sound. Available on Windows, Linux and Mac OS, the game is adapted for different screen sizes and is suitable for users of all experience levels, including the visually impaired.
